<h2>Conjunction ( ∧ )</h2>
<p>
Let p and q be propositions. The conjunction of p and q, denoted by <strong>p ∧ q</strong>, 
is the proposition “p and q.” The conjunction <strong>p ∧ q</strong> is true when both p and q are true 
and is false otherwise.
</p>
