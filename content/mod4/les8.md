<h2>Converse</h2>
<p>The converse of a conditional statement <strong>p → q</strong> is the statement <strong>q → p</strong>. It switches the hypothesis and conclusion.</p>

<div style="background:#eef; padding:15px; border-left:4px solid var(--primary); margin: 10px 0;">
    <p><strong>Example:</strong></p>
    <p>Original: If it rains (p), then the ground is wet (q). → p → q</p>
    <p>Converse: If the ground is wet (q), then it rains (p). → q → p</p>
</div>
