<h2>Inverse</h2>
<p>The inverse of a conditional statement <strong>p → q</strong> is <strong>¬p → ¬q</strong>. It negates both the hypothesis and the conclusion.</p>

<div style="background:#eef; padding:15px; border-left:4px solid var(--primary); margin: 10px 0;">
    <p><strong>Example:</strong></p>
    <p>Original: If it rains (p), then the ground is wet (q).</p>
    <p>Inverse: If it does not rain (¬p), then the ground is not wet (¬q).</p>
</div>
