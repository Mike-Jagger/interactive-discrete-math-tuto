<h2>Introduction to Propositions</h2>
<p>A proposition is a sentence that declares a fact, and it is either true or false, but not both. Thus, propositions are declarative sentences.</p>
<div style="background:#eef; padding:15px; border-left:4px solid var(--primary); margin: 10px 0;">
<strong>Examples:</strong><br>
- The Sun rises in the east.<br>
- The Pacific ocean is the smallest ocean in the world.<br>
- All squares are rectangles.
</div>
<p>Propositions are <strong>NOT</strong>; questions, commands, requests, suggestions, expressions or the likes.</p>
